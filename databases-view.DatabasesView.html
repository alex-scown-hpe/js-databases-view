Class: DatabasesView

databases-view/js/databases-view. DatabasesView

<virtual> new DatabasesView(options)

View for showing and selecting HP Haven OnDemand resources. This is an abstract class and must be supplied with templates for databases and categories. In addition, six primitive operations must be implemented:

  • check
  • uncheck
  • enable
  • disable
  • determinate
  • indeterminate
